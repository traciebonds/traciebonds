The Book

9 essential practices for hearing HIS voice.

Many struggle to recognize when God is speaking. You wonder if that nudge was from Him or simply your own thoughts.

In GOD, I WANT TO HEAR YOU, I share 9 essential practices, personal testimonies, reflection questions, and powerful prayers that can transform how you hear and communicate with God.

Whether you are new to faith or have walked with Jesus for years, these practices will help you hear HIS voice more clearly.

About Tracie

Biblical mindset coach. Author. Speaker.

Tracie Bonds is a biblical mindset coach, author, speaker, and host of The Daily Prayer Call. She helps guide believers to pray, listen, and obey.

I really do believe that the voice of God is the most powerful, available, and underused gift in the life of a believer. What you will find here is the overflow of more than thirteen years of gathering with prayer warriors, learning to listen, and learning to obey.
