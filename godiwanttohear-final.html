God is Speaking.
Can You Hear Him?

Hearing God is vital to obeying Him. And obeying Him is how we walk in the abundant, Spirit-filled life He promises. This book was written to help every believer grow deeper in their intimacy with Jesus and more confident in recognizing His voice.

If you have ever wondered whether God still speaks to ordinary people, whether you could learn to recognize His voice, and whether He would speak to you, the answer is yes. And this book was written to show you how.

The truth is, most Christians don't even know what they are missing. They assume this is just what the Christian life feels like, going through the motions, hoping God shows up. But there is so much more available to you.

In GOD, I WANT TO HEAR YOU, Tracie Bonds shares nine essential practices rooted in Scripture that will transform how you hear and communicate with God.

Are You Being Discipled?
According to Barna Group, America's leading faith research organization, nearly 4 in 10 Christians are not engaged in discipleship in any form.

Yet discipleship was the very model Jesus used to prepare and equip His followers. For three years He walked with His disciples, teaching them to know Him, hear Him, and obey Him. Before ascending to heaven He gave them the Great Commission: "Go into all the world and make disciples, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit." Discipleship was never optional. It was the plan.

GOD, I WANT TO HEAR YOU was written for every believer who is ready to go deeper.

Tracie Bonds is a Christian author, speaker, entrepreneur, and spiritual strategy coach who teaches believers how to hear God's voice and apply His direction to their lives.

Her journey from $232,000 in IRS debt and a home in foreclosure to earning more than $12 million over a seventeen-year network marketing career was not built on conventional business strategies. It was built on learning to hear and obey God's voice.

For more than thirteen years, Tracie has hosted The Daily Prayer Call, a free weekday gathering where believers come together to seek God and learn to hear His voice.

Tracie lives in Franklin, Tennessee and enjoys spending time with her children and grandchildren, traveling, and documenting her own God stories.
